About Kasia

Your best web developer

Hi! I am na aspiring front end developer with passion for good quality code and attention to detail. I am an organized and responsible person, caring about the quality of my performance. I find myself working very well both independently and in a team.

I am a student of Warsaw University of Technology majoring in biomedical engineering. This led me to gain sufficient knowledge in a range of technical and scientific fields. I enjoy travelling not only as a hobby, byt also as a way to further educate myself. During the student exchange in the Erasmus programme at the Danish Technical University I had the opportunity to take part in various projects and improve my knowledge of English, both in speech and in writing. During my participation in ATHENS programme I had an opportunity to meet students from all over Europe and collaborated with them on assigned projects.

For the last two years I have been consistently improving my level of German by taking part in language courses and solving tests.

I do have a very particular set of skills

I have started my web development journey in the 6th grade of primary school coding my first website in HTML. Later on I learnt CSS to beautify my teenage blog. Last year I participated in Code Carrots JavaScript course, which gave me good foundation of JS knowledge. I learnt about DOM, object oriented programming, GIT. I know now the basics of jQuery and even a little bit of AngularJS. I am able to write my projects' documentation using Github's gist and markdown.

During April-August 2016 I worked as a front-end intern at CodiLime, where I joined a team developing a web application using CoffeeScript, HTML5, Sass, AngularJS. I have gained sufficient knowledge of AngularJS and experience writing unit tests using Jasmine. I am now familiar with GIT workflow and the concept of feature branches as well as with Atlassian tools sucha as Jira, Confluence, Bitbucket. I learnt good coding practices during code reviews.

I am a fast learner, my willingness to learn and discover new things drives me to improve and challenge myself constantly. I work efficiently under stress and organise my time well.
